I am co-organizing a Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS) 2026 workshop! The workshop is Lab-to-Production. This year ICRA accepted only 44% of the proposed workshops, and it remains a leading venue for embodied intelligence, currently ranking as the world’s #8 robotics conference by h5-index. This workshop aims to steer state-of-the-art robotics research toward solving real-world deployment and production challenges.
