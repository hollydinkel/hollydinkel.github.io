I am co-organizing a Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS) 2026 workshop! The workshop is [**Lab-to-Production**](https://robotics-workshop.github.io/l2p-rss26/). This year ICRA accepted only 44% of the proposed workshops, and it remains a leading venue for embodied intelligence, currently ranking as the world's [#8 robotics conference](https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=top_venues&hl=en&vq=eng_robotics) by h5-index. This workshop aims to steer state-of-the-art robotics research toward solving real-world deployment and production challenges.

**Submit your extended abstract:** ➡️ [L2P Workshop OpenReview](https://openreview.net/forum?id=ccQtGBR6XKE)

This event would not be possible without the attention and care of co-organizers [Bibit Bianchini](http://www.bianchini-love.com/bibit), [Xiao Liu](https://www.xiao-liu.me/), [Kris Hauser](hhttps://kkhauser.web.illinois.edu/), [Youngwoon Lee](https://youngwoon.github.io/), [Alessio Caporali](https://www.unibo.it/sitoweb/alessio.caporali/en), [Simon Manschitz](https://www.ias.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/Team/SimonManschitz), [Eric Heiden](https://eric-heiden.com/), and [Kelsey Allen](https://k-r-allen.github.io/).
